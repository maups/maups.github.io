Research

Biometrics

Works with biometric recognition since 2005. Has experience in different identification approaches such as face, fingerprint and ear recognition. In 2007, his work was acknowledged as the best undergraduate research project at the Brazilian Symposium on Computer Graphics and Image Processing (SIBGRAPI). In 2014, his D.Sc. thesis was acknowledged as the third best thesis in Computer Science at the Brazilian Computer Society Congress (CSBC).

3D Reconstruction

Designed and developed 3D reconstruction tools for face biometrics and cultural heritage preservation purposes. Corroborated neuropsychology works by quantitatively showing that half-frontal face images have more information than their frontal or profile versions. Worked in the scanning and construction of digital models of statues from a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage site.

Deep Learning

Most recent passion for research and competition. Teamed up with Dr. Rodrigo Minetto and Dr. Sudeep Sarkar to participate in IARPA's Functional Map of the World challenge on satellite image understanding, finishing in third place (official result). Also competed on Intel's Movidius Challenge to run deep networks on its recently launched Neural Compute Stick, finishing in second place.
